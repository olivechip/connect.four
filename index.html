Rules

A player will be chosen randomly to go first.

Player 1 and Player 2 alternate turns.

On each turn, a piece is dropped down a column until a player gets four-in-a-row to win the game.

Horizontal, vertical, or diagonal alignments all work.

If the board fills with no winner, the game results in a tie.

Good luck!
